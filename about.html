About Wanderly

Wanderly is a student-built travel recommendation site designed to help users discover destinations tailored to their interests. Our goal is to provide clear descriptions, beautiful imagery, and easy navigation to plan memorable trips.

Our Mission

To make travel discovery enjoyable, simple, inspiring, and accessible — helping users find the right destination quickly.

Our Team

Small team of passionate students and developers building intuitive web experiences.
